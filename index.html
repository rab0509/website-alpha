My Background

I have a somewhat of a diverse background. I have a B.S. in Math from the University of the District of Columbia. I have worked as a COBOL programmer (back in the day!) for Booz-Allen and Hamilton, a digital IC design engineer for several high-tech companies, a volunteer with St.Vincent de Paul, and a K-12 Substitute Teacher with the Chandler Unified School District.

Why Programming?

What fascinates me about programming is how the scope of software development, data management, and data security has come to touch almost every fabric of our lives today. The field of software development offers a myriad of opportunities today for programmers who are motivated to tackle the challenges facing an ever-more inter-connected world. I want to be a software developer who is a problem solver helping meet the challenges of this industry.
